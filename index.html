Tour Reflection & Learnings

Overall Experience (Cebu-Bohol)

The Cebu-Bohol Educational Tour (November 19-22, 2025) was an excellent and very accommodating experience. The bus ride was comfortable, and the tour guides were highly commended for being very accommodating and very good. My overall experience was accommodating and the best tour.

*Scanned Journal Entry - Overall Thoughts*

Visit 1: WORLDTECH INFORMATION SOLUTIONS, INC.

Date: November 19, 2025 | Facilitator: Elizabeth Aquiza

I was amazed by the company's Cisco equipments and abundance of servers. The facility was well-ventilated and aesthetically pleasing.

Key Learnings:

  • The host's explanation of hacking and how culprits use it to defraud banks was highly educational.
  • This reinforced the critical need for better technology securing in all professions.
  • The experience inspired me to pursue more certifications in Cyber Security, recognizing that cyber security is everyone's responsibility and part of life.

*Scanned Journal Entry - WORLDTECH Learnings*

Visit 2: RIVAN IT Cebu

Date: November 20, 2025 | Facilitator: Vincent Victor

The visit to RIVAN IT focused on their approach to client solutions and technical support. It provided insight into the operational side of a smaller, dedicated IT service company.

Key Takeaway:

  • Importance of clear communication in client-facing IT roles.
  • Understanding the lifecycle of providing technical solutions from consultation to implementation.

Scanned Journal Entry - RIVAN IT Learnings

Visit 3: CODECHUM

Date: November 20, 2025 | Facilitator: Xavier David Maranga

The office environment was well-ventilated and aesthetically pleasing, creating a welcoming atmosphere. The host, Sir Xavier, was described as well-spoken.

Key Learnings:

  • The main point of CODECHUM is to make programming interesting, fun, and simple.
  • Observed the use of an AI grading system for automatic code checking.
  • The visit emphasized the importance of continuous learning: "Never stop learning. Keep moving forward.".
  • As an IT student, I realized the need to pursue things I am passionate about.

Visit 4: MATA Technologies, Inc.

Date: November 21, 2025 | Facilitator: Suzzette Cabinero

The MATA Technologies visit provided insight into business operations and the integration of technology in modern enterprises. It highlighted different career paths within the IT field beyond development.

Key Takeaway:

  • Understanding the role of IT in business strategy and management.
  • Exposure to various departments within a large technology firm.
